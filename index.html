I am an international student from China. I graduated from the University of Arizona in Management Information Systems. I also minored in Statistics and Data Science to explore data-related knowledge. These education experiences improved my learning ability and promoted my enthusiasm towards a new field. Now I am pursuing my master's degree at the University of Michigan School of Information in UX Research and Design track.


I tried to use the sketch to finish an undergraduate course related to user experience, which is a rough start to my interaction design field. I also use procreate and photoshop to make some calligraphy and photos. My design philosophy is simple and attractive, sustainable. I think good design should attract people and must be sustainable. Additionally, simple has the power to draw attention and keep a memory.
